How Can YOU Make a Difference?

Ways You Can Help:

There are many ways to help the environment but here are a few of the easiest. First of all, make sure to use less water. Secondly, reduce the amount of electricity you use. Next, eat less meat. This doesn't mean you need to become a vegetarian but try to cut down on the amount of meat you eat. Also, use less plastic. Plastic is killing marine life all around the planet. Lastly, try to emit less pollution. You can do this by walking, biking, or even carpooling with a friend! These arre only a few ways you can help.

It doesn't matter your age, gender, race, etc. even the slightest help to reduce climate change can make wonderous changes in the world. It's never too late. Start today and help your world.
