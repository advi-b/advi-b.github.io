Climate Change

What is Climate Change?

Climate Change is change in rainfall, temperature, and other measures in climate that happen over many decades or longer

img src= https://static.thenounproject.com/png/1030201-200.png alt = Climate Change Icon>

Causes of Climate Change

Water vapor, Carbon Dioxide, Methane, and more greenhouse gases are causing the temperature on Earth to become hotter. This is called the Greenhouse Effect. All these gases cause heat to trap in the Earth and the temperatures to rise.

Effects of Climate Change

  1. Wildfires will burn up to eight times as much land by 2100
  2. 100% of reefs will be extinct by 2050
  3. More droughts will make dry regions every more drier
  4. Air pollution will increase which could become a major health hazard
  5. Hunger will increase by 20 million by 2050
